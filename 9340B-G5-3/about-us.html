About Us

We are Team Balangay, a group of SCIS students in Saint Louis University, Baguio City who aims to raise awareness on inclusion, human rights, and religious tolerance and to promote equality, respect, peace and order regardless of gender, religion and culture with the means of creating a website under the course of Web Technologies and under the supervision of our instructor, Sir Roderick Makil.
