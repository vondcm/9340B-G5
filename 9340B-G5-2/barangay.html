About Brgy. Imelda Marcos

Imelda Marcos is located at Marcos Highway, Baguio City. It was named after the wife of the late and former president of the Philippines, Mr. Ferdinand Emmanuel Edralin Marcos. Mrs. Imelda Remedios Visitación Romuáldez-Marcos, now 88 years of age, is considered one of the richest and most powerful woman in her time. Coming from a poor family, she worked her way up from becoming a saleslady at a music store to becoming a beauty queen before meeting the late Mr. Ferdinand Marcos. There are no details as to why the barangay was named after the former first lady, however, if it was not the case, the barangay would have been called "La Salle".

Mr. Ernesto Laguardia has been the barangay captain since 2002. The barangay has a small population (1,513 as of 2016). There are 2 routes to take to go to the barangay. One is via Route 54 (27 mins, 2.0 km) and the other is via Military Cutoff Rd. (34 mins, 2.6 km).

Vision:

We want to be the foremost environmental friendly barangay giving equal opportunities through sustainable social and economic development programs.

Mission:

A united barangay committed to the delivery of basic services, through institutionalized and self-sustaining undertakings for the social and economic upliftment of our constituents.
