About Brgy. Imelda Marcos

There are 129 barangays in Baguio City and one of them is the barangay Imelda Marcos. It was named after the wife of the late and former president of the Philippines, Mr. Ferdinand Emmanuel Edralin Marcos. Mrs. Imelda Remedios Visitación Romuáldez-Marcos, now 88 years of age, is a woman who is rich, powerful, and owns thousand pairs of shoes. But little did the people know that Mrs. Imelda Marcos came from a poor family and worked her way up from becoming a saleslady at a music store to becoming a beauty queen before meeting the late Mr. Ferdinand Marcos. There are no details as to why the barangay was named after the former first lady Imelda Marcos, however, if it was not the case, the barangay would have been called "La Salle".

Imelda Marcos is located at Marcos Highway, Baguio City. Mr. Ernesto Laguardia has been the barangay captain since 2002. The barangay has a small population (1,513 as of 2016). There are 2 routes to take to go to the barangay. One is via Route 54 (27 mins, 2.0 km) and the other is via Military Cutoff Rd. (34 mins, 2.6 km).

Vision:

We want to be the foremost environmental friendly barangay giving equal opportunities through sustainable social and economic development programs.

Mission:

A united barangay committed to the delivery of basic services, through institutionalized and self-sustaining undertakings for the social and economic upliftment of our constituents.
